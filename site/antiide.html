What AntiIDE is

Finally see the code you're responsible for.

AntiIDE reads your whole repository and turns it into something you can actually understand — the map, the health, the docs, the review. You never write code into it: it's read-only, it never touches your files, and nothing leaves your machine.

  • Onboard in hours, not weeks. See how a codebase fits together instead of reading it file by file.
  • Know what a change will break before you touch it — every file's dependents, mapped.
