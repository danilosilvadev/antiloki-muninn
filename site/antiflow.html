What AntiFlow gives you

The assembly line, the laws, the sealed record.

01 · the assembly line
Architecture before code. Always.
The plan is drawn and approved before a line exists. Then one file at a time — written, really built, really tested, in a sealed sandbox. A bad change stops at the gate, gets fixed, resumes.

02 · the laws
Rules the AI can't break.
Your engineering rules live as data, enforced by the machine — triggers, gates, static checks. Not a prompt the model might ignore: a wall it physically hits. Process without memory.
