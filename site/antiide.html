Privileged session

Required to halt the driver, archive cards, reject contracts, or change cost caps. Paste the raw substrate god key from .antiloki/auth/dev.key. The BE exchanges it for a 15-minute session token; the raw key is never stored in your browser.

Why short-lived? If your browser is compromised (rogue extension, XSS), the attacker can read localStorage. A 15-min session bounds the blast radius; the raw key on disk stays the only durable source of trust.
