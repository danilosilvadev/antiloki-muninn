You hold the reins

Your models. Your money. Your verdicts.

Cost shows your real spend — your provider's own numbers, every call itemized, and a daily cap per tier that refuses calls past the line. Tagging lets you rate the AI's answers; your verdict always outranks the AI's, and the router picks better next time.

  • Cap the daily spend. Set a limit per tier; at the line, paid calls stop and cached answers stay free.
  • Real numbers, not estimates. Spend is pulled live from your own provider account.
  • Compare models fairly. Every call draws one model at random from your bank, so you see them on the same work.
  • Your rating wins. When you and the AI judge disagree, your verdict counts more — always.
  • Teach the router. One click, plus an optional reason, becomes training data that improves the next answer.
